Your Trusted Appliance Repair

Cape Town Appliance Repairs, also trading as Fixman, has been providing professional on-site appliance repair services for over 15 years. We specialize in fixing washing machines, tumble dryers, ovens, fridges, dishwashers, Speed Queen units, and all major household appliances across Cape Town and surrounding areas. Our commitment is to provide reliable, same-day service with a focus on quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.
